MICHAEL'S FONTS

PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

Michael's Fonts ("I", "me") operates michaelsfonts.com ("Website"). This Privacy Policy explains what data I collect and how I handle it. Short version: I collect almost nothing.

2. Data I Collect

I do not collect, store, or process personal data on this Website. Specifically:

• No account creation or login required

• No cookies used for tracking or analytics

• No email addresses collected on the Website

• No browsing behavior tracked

• No third-party analytics or advertising scripts

3. Payment Processing

All purchases are processed by Stripe (stripe.com). When you make a purchase, Stripe collects your payment information (card number, billing address, email) directly. I do not see or store your full card details. Stripe's handling of your data is governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.

4. Information I Receive from Stripe

After a purchase, Stripe provides me with:

• Your email address (for font file delivery)

• Transaction amount and date

• Partial card information (last 4 digits only)

I use this information solely to deliver your purchased font files via email and respond to support inquiries. I do not sell, share, or use this data for marketing.

5. Cookies

This Website does not use cookies. No first-party cookies, no third-party cookies, no tracking pixels.

6. Hosting

This Website is hosted on Netlify. Netlify may collect basic server logs (IP addresses, browser type, access times) as part of their infrastructure. See Netlify's Privacy Policy for details.

7. Email Communication

If you email me at mail@michaelsfonts.com, I will use your email address only to respond to your inquiry. I do not add you to any mailing list.

8. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy: mail@michaelsfonts.com
